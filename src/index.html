Frequently asked questions

How is this different from ChatGPT?

While ChatGPT is a powerful language model capable of generating text formats, it primarily operates on a passive basis, requiring specific prompts to produce desired outputs. In contrast, Para is designed to be active. It doesn't merely respond to queries; it actively engages with the user by suggesting, updating, and synthesizing information. This active approach transforms Para into a virtual research advisor, guiding users through the entire research process rather than simply providing information upon request. Essentially, while ChatGPT is a tool, Para is a collaborative partner.

What can Para do?

Para adapts to each individual user by learning about their research field, prior works, research group, and publications.
Para can suggest relevant papers and conferences, summarize content, read and analyze papers, collect and analyze data, design and refine experiments, write and present findings, and propose next tasks in your research and/or development cycle.
